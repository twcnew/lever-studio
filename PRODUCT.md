# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Seed to Series B B2B founders and GTM leaders with a technical product, a sales team ready to take meetings, and no predictable pipeline engine yet.

## Product Purpose

Position Lever as a solo AI-native GTM engineer who builds systems clients own — not an agency retainer or another tool login.

## Brand Personality

Expert, direct, craft-forward. Playful through visual assets (painting XIX + ASCII), not through SaaS clichés.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS landing pages (repeated eyebrows, identical card grids, beam borders, orbit animations). Frontal/ColdIQ copy clones. Multiple visual languages on one page.

## Design Principles

1. Hero is the cinematic exception; everything below is one modular Clay-like system.
2. Show the system through use cases and product-shaped visuals, not abstract metrics.
3. One reveal animation system for all sections below the hero.
4. Assets live in named slots until painting/ASCII finals ship.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on body text. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all reveals and hero intro.
